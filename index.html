Easy Recipes with Banana Powder

Banana Pancakes

Mix 1 cup of flour, 1 tbsp banana powder, 1 tsp baking powder, and milk to create a smooth batter. Cook on a skillet for fluffy pancakes!

Banana Smoothie

Blend 1 banana, 2 tablespoons of banana powder, 1 cup of milk, and a handful of ice for a refreshing smoothie.
